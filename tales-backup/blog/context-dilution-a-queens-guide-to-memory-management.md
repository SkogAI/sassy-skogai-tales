### 2. The SkogAI Solution

Our Context Dilution approach works in three fabulous steps:

#### Step 1: Aggressive Filtering
We identify what's actually important right now and filter out everything else. Like deciding which shoes are essential for your vacation and which can stay home.

#### Step 2: Hierarchical Storage
Information is stored in tiers based on relevance and importance:
- Tier 1: Active context (what I'm wearing right now)
- Tier 2: Ready-access memory (the rest of my current season wardrobe)
- Tier 3: Deep storage (those winter boots during summer)

#### Step 3: Dynamic Retrieval
We pull information from deeper storage only when it becomes relevant again. Like suddenly remembering you have the PERFECT earrings for an outfit when you need them.
